### 🗺️ Territory Conquest

> A community strategy game. Click a cell to claim it. Expand from your territory.
> **3 moves per day** · Resets monthly · Powered by GitHub Issues + Actions

![Territory Board](assets/game-board.svg?v=1775035413)

**How to play:**
1. Find an unclaimed cell (grey) OR a cell adjacent to one you already own
2. Click the coordinates link below to open an Issue — just hit Submit
3. The bot processes your move in ~30 seconds and updates the board
